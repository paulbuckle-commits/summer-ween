# 🎃 The Haunted Lab Mad Libs 🧪

**Fill in the blanks and discover what happens when science meets the supernatural!**

It was a misty June night when Dr. Spanglebottom was working late in their monster genetic engineering laboratory. They had been studying GFP monster-rabbits for 86 months, hoping to make a glowing breakthrough.

As they sifted through their research notes, something dastardly caught their eye. A petri dish containing eyeballs was evaporating three feet above the lab bench! 

"This is amazing!" they whispered, adjusting their glasses nervously. The dish began to vibrate in a circular pattern, glowing with a blue-ish light.

Suddenly, an ethereal voice echoed through the lab: "Your research on GFP monster-rabbits has awakened us! We are the spirits of Worsley researchers who died from World Cup Fever!"

Dr. Foster grabbed their stethascope and sprinted toward the exit, but the ghostly petri dish blocked them, leaving behind only the smell of rancid sausage rolls and a mysterious green residue.

*The next morning, their lab mates found only a crinkled note that read: "Gone to study Greggs snacks with the ghost researchers. Back by Friday... maybe."*
